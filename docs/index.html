背景：本頁面為香港教育大學「CHI3565 漢語修辭學」客席講堂的輔助教材，並為未來的「數碼人文 (Digital Humanities)」課程提供實例示範與研究平台。Context: This page serves as supplementary material for the guest lecture of "CHI3565 Chinese Rhetoric" at EdUHK, and as a practical demonstration and research platform for future Digital Humanities courses.
摘要：本項目運用數碼人文方法，以數據為本對 1986 至 2025 年間近千首香港發行的粵語流行歌進行計量分析。系統結合 NLP、詞庫模型分析 (如 LIWC) 及 AI 技術，從語音（聲調走向、押韻、疊字）、用詞（文言/口語比例、詞彙多樣性、人稱代詞）及內容（主題情感、地區投射）三大維度，科學化地探討香港流行音樂歌詞文本的演變特徵。Abstract: This project applies Digital Humanities methods for a data-driven quantitative analysis of nearly a thousand Cantonese popular songs distributed in Hong Kong from 1986 to 2025. Combining NLP, lexicon-based models (e.g. LIWC), and AI techniques, the system explores the evolution of lyrics across phonetics (tone movements, rhymes, reduplication), lexis (register, lexical diversity, pronoun usage), and themes (emotions, geographic projections).

結論與研究視角 Conclusions & Research Perspectives

比對 1986-2025 年的圖表走勢與本課堂提出的研究假設，我們能歸納出以下跨時代的語言及文學現象：By examining the trends from 1986 to 2025 against the hypotheses introduced in class, we can observe the following diachronic linguistic and literary phenomena:
  • 語音的持續與變化：由「旋律要求」驅使的語言特徵恆定不變（如為配合 0243 走向，相鄰聲調的「高聲調大跳躍」比例長期維持極低），這印證了「先曲後詞」的生產限制（假設 1 成立）。相對而言，不影響旋律結構的押韻比例逐漸提高（近十年接近 80%），而疊字作爲修辭手法未有消減。Phonetic Constraints & Changes: Features driven by melody restrictions remain constant (e.g., low ratio of large tonal jumps due to the constraints of the 0243 melodic contour), confirming the "melody-first" production (Hypothesis 1 verified). Meanwhile, "free" features like rhymes have gradually increased (near 80% recently) and reduplication remains prevalent.
  • 語體與修辭風格演化：隨着流行文化加速及時代變遷，宏觀數據顯示歌詞寫作減少了雕琢與文言、莊重成份，變得更貼近口語化與日常體（假設 3 成立）。同時，整體詞彙多樣性 (Type-Token Ratio, TTR) 及異常詞數量均反映有輕微下降趨勢。Evolution of Register & Rhetoric: Macro data shows a reduction in elaborate, classical, or formal vocabulary, moving closer to spoken Cantonese and everyday language (Hypothesis 3 verified). Overall lexical diversity (TTR) and rare vocabulary exhibit a slight downward trend.
  • 主題意識與國際化進程：出乎意料地，數據圖表顯示大部份廣東歌至今依然以浪漫愛情及失戀為絕對主要題材，並未如日本流行文化般出現明顯「由戀愛轉向自我肯定」的大規模轉移（假設 6 暫不成立）。在地名引用上，多局限於香港、台灣、日本及西方旅遊熱點，未見因國際視野提升而令文本發生深層的「主題國際化」（假設 5 暫不成立）。Thematic Focus & Internationalization: Contrary to expectations, Cantonese lyrics remain overwhelmingly dominated by romantic love and heartbreak themes, lacking the paradigm shift toward "self-affirmation" seen in Japanese pop (Hypothesis 6 unsubstantiated). Geographic references remain localized (Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, Europe), without deep thematic internationalization (Hypothesis 5 unsubstantiated).
  • 情感波動：從整套歌詞的情感分析分數 (Sentiment) 可見，歷年總體趨平穩，惟隨年代推進呈輕微而緩慢的向下（悲觀）趨勢。在晝夜意象比例上，寫「日」的歌曲數量仍持續多於寫「夜」。Sentiment Fluctuations: Overall sentiment scores remain relatively stable but indicate a slight, gradual downward (more pessimistic) trend over the decades. Regarding day/night imagery, songs referencing "day" still consistently outnumber those referencing "night".
藉由上述數據視覺化儀表板可見，數碼人文 (Digital Humanities) 方法能處理傳統文學分析難以涵蓋的大規模語料，發掘文本底層的語言特質與時代情感，並讓文科學生能通過 AI 輔助獨立進行大型文本探索與實證研究。As demonstrated by the dashboard, Digital Humanities methods can process large-scale corpora beyond the reach of traditional literary analysis, uncovering underlying linguistic traits and temporal sentiments. It empowers humanities students to conduct independent, large-scale textual exploration and empirical research with AI assistance.

鳴謝與使用工具 Acknowledgements & Tools

本研究項目使用了多種開源工具及自然語言處理（NLP）資源，特此鳴謝：This research project utilizes various open-source tools and Natural Language Processing (NLP) resources. We would like to acknowledge:
  • Cantonese Detect — 用於辨識歌詞中的粵語成份與書面語比例。 (Used to detect the proportion of explicit Cantonese components vs. Standard Written Chinese.)
  • spaCy — 工業級核心 NLP 庫，用於實體命名識別（Named Entity Recognition），以擷取地名。 (Industrial-strength NLP library used for Named Entity Recognition (NER) to extract geographical locations.)
  • LIWC-Canto / LIWC — 運用包含四萬多詞條的廣東話分類詞庫進行文本主題（如愛、日、夜）及情感分析。 (Applied for thematic (e.g. Love, Day, Night) and sentiment analysis using a Cantonese categorized lexicon of over 40,000 words.)
  • ToJyutping — 用於將漢字轉化為粵拼（Jyutping），協助進行聲調走向與押韻計量。 (Used to convert Chinese characters into Jyutping for tone movement and rhyme computations.)
